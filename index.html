Rock Paper Scissors

The most popular hand game

According to the World Rock Paper Scissors Association, the Rock Paper scissors game is the oldest hand game ever played, dating back to 206 BC - 220 AD and became a global success over the last two centuries, primarily because of its simplicity, being a game that can be easily understood no matter the audience.

It's not all luck

Since each of the three options have each a 1/3 win chance, none of them is better than the other, so that would mean it's impossible to have a consistent winning strategy.
However, a study made in 2014 by two chinese researchers proved that there are some patterns that can be exploited.
